<h1 align="center">STUDENT TRAINING AGREEMENT:</h1>


<br><br>
 
This Student Training Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between **Mr. Sam Rohan** ("Mentor/Admin") and the enrolling individual ("Student"). This Agreement governs the Student's participation in skill development training programs, workshops, courses, and services provided by the Mentor. Collectively, the Mentor and Student may be referred to as the "Parties."

<br>

**1. Acknowledgment:** By enrolling in any training program, the Student confirms they have read, understood, and agreed to the terms outlined in this Agreement. This legally binding contract establishes the expectations, rights, and responsibilities of both Parties throughout the duration of the Training Program.

<br>

**2. Definitions:**

* **Training Program:** A structured learning initiative including live classes, pre-recorded modules, workshops, and practical assignments conducted by the Mentor.
* **Course Materials:** Educational content such as documents, presentations, source code, multimedia content, software, and proprietary tools provided to aid student learning.

<br>

**3. Provision of Services:**

1. **Delivery:** The Mentor will make every reasonable effort to conduct the Training Program according to the advertised syllabus, ensuring consistent instructional quality and relevance.

2. **Format:** Training is generally provided online, with occasional on-premise workshops as exceptions. Programs may include live or recorded sessions, collaborative projects, and evaluations.

3. **Modifications:** The Mentor reserves the right to adjust the training schedule, curriculum, instructors, and assessment methods as necessary. Any changes will be communicated when possible to reduce inconvenience.

4. **Rescheduling:** In the event of disruptions caused by unforeseen circumstances (e.g., technical failures, emergencies), affected sessions will be rescheduled at a mutually convenient time.

<br>

**4. Mentor Responsibilities:**

The Mentor commits to:

1. Appoint qualified trainers to conduct the Training Program.

2. Provide complete access to necessary learning resources.

3. Develop and evaluate assignments, assessments, and projects to measure progress.

4. Deliver timely and constructive feedback to the Student.

5. Issue a certificate of completion to eligible Students who meet the course requirements.

<br>

**5. Student Responsibilities:**

Students agree to:

1. Attend all sessions punctually and consistently.

2. Engage actively in discussions, exercises, and assignments.

3. Complete and submit assessments within specified deadlines.

4. Follow the rules and conduct guidelines set by the Mentor.

5. Keep all proprietary and confidential information private.

6. Notify the Mentor in advance of any absences or inability to meet deadlines.

<br>

**6. Code of Conduct:** All Students are expected to behave respectfully and professionally:

* Treat trainers, peers, and staff with courtesy.

* Avoid inappropriate behavior, including harassment or academic dishonesty.

* Maintain a collaborative and inclusive learning environment.

**Violation of these rules may lead to expulsion from the program, forfeiture of certification, or legal consequences.**

<br>

**7. Fees and Payment:**

1. **Fee Structure:** The fee varies depending on course complexity, content, delivery format, student category, and resource requirements.

2. **Payment Methods:** UPI, bank transfer, or cash.

3. **Payment Terms:** Either full payment or approved installment options.

4. **Refund Policy:** No refunds are issued after the training begins. Students are encouraged to attend demo sessions and clarify all doubts before enrolling.

<br>

**8. Intellectual Property:**

1. All Course Materials remain the exclusive intellectual property of the Mentor.

2. Students may use these materials for personal study only.

3. Reproduction, sharing, or commercial use of any training materials is strictly prohibited.

<br>

**9. Confidentiality:** Students agree to keep all non-public information confidential. This includes training strategies, project specifications, proprietary technologies, and private communications.

<br>

**10. Data Privacy and Security:**

1. Student data is collected solely with consent and used only for training-related purposes.

2. The Mentor adheres to strong IT security practices.

3. No personal data will be sold or shared without explicit permission, except as required by law.

4. Data is deleted after course completion and is not archived or recoverable.

<br>

**11. Limitation of Liability:** The Mentor shall not be liable for indirect or consequential damages resulting from participation in the training. The total liability shall not exceed the fees paid by the Student.

<br>

**12. Defamation:** False or defamatory remarks directed at the Mentor, team, or program materials may result in expulsion and legal action. Students are encouraged to communicate concerns respectfully and use formal feedback channels.

<br>
 
**13. Dispute Resolution:**

1. **Mediation First:** Disputes will first be addressed through mutual dialogue and mediation.

2. **Arbitration Next:** Unresolved issues will be handled through binding arbitration in accordance with the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996. The jurisdiction and legal language will be as agreed by both Parties.

<br>

**14. International Students:**

Students enrolling from outside India agree to:

1. Participate in virtual legal or mediation proceedings if necessary.

2. Accept Indian jurisdiction for legal matters related to the Training Program.

3. Provide appropriate documentation for any legal claims or disputes.

<br>

**15. Final Acceptance:**

By registering for the Training Program and participating in any orientation or demo sessions, the Student confirms that they:

* Have understood and agreed to this Agreement.
* Acknowledge the rules, responsibilities, and limitations described herein.
* Are fully informed about the Training Program and willingly accept its terms.
